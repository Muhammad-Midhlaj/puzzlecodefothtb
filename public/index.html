Help for Puzzle Code

Your goal is to collect all the gold coins using your robot.

Tell your robot what to do by writing a "program" in the dark text box (the right side of the dark text box).

To run your program, click the Run! button.

You can edit your program by clicking the Reset button.

If you want to run your program, one step at a time, press the Step button.




If you have any questions, or suggestions on how to improve these instructions, please email me at mikegagnon@gmail.com
