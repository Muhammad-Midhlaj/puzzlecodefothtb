How to play Puzzle Code

Try to collect all the gold coins using your blue robot.

You cannot move your robot using your mouse or keyboard.

Instead, you must tell your robot what to do by writing a "program".

A program is just a list of instructions that your robot will follow exactly.

Write your instructions in the text box on the right side.

To run your program, click the Run! button.

You can edit your program by clicking the Reset button.

If you want to run your program, one step at a time, press the Step button.
